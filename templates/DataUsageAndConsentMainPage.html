AI-Assisted Medical Diagnosis

  • CancerVision is an advanced AI-powered clinical decision support platform designed to assist healthcare professionals in the detection and subtype classification of cancer across multiple imaging modalities, including CT, MRI, X-ray, Mammography, and Dermoscopy.
  • The platform utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning architectures, including lightweight and high-capacity convolutional neural networks, to analyze medical imaging data and generate predictive insights. These insights are intended to support medical evaluation and improve diagnostic efficiency.
  • CancerVision does not replace professional medical judgment, clinical evaluation, radiological interpretation, or pathological confirmation.
Important Medical Disclaimer :
  • CancerVision functions strictly as a clinical decision support tool. It is designed to augment, not substitute, the expertise of licensed physicians, radiologists, or other qualified healthcare professionals.
  • All AI-generated findings, including detection results, subtype classifications, confidence scores, Grad-CAM visualizations, and automated recommendations, must be independently reviewed, interpreted, and validated by a licensed medical professional before any medical decision is made.
  • The platform is not intended for emergency use and should not be relied upon as the sole basis for diagnosis or treatment planning.

Data Collected

  • To provide AI-powered medical analysis, CancerVision processes the following types of data:

Medical Images

Medical imaging files including DICOM, PNG, JPG/JPEG, TIFF, and multi-slice CT/MRI stacks from CT, MRI, Mammography, X-ray, and Dermoscopy studies. Image slices and related metadata may be processed for analysis.

Patient Metadata

Patient details entered by the uploader, including name, age/date of birth, gender, ID (if provided), exam date, anatomical region, and clinical indication. Optional fields must be explicitly provided by the user.

AI Analysis Outputs

Model predictions, subtype classifications, confidence scores, lesion coordinates, and Grad-CAM heatmaps generated during analysis.

  • Privacy & Security : Personally identifiable information (PII) is encrypted or anonymized where applicable. For security, audit logging, and platform integrity, the system may record upload timestamps, uploader account identifiers, session activity, and limited device/browser information. No personally identifiable health records are stored beyond the active session unless required for registered report access.

Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

1

Image Ingestion & Validation :

Medical images (including DICOM and other supported formats) are securely received, validated for integrity, and prepared for analysis.

2

Image Preprocessing :

Images are normalized, resized, and standardized before being fed into CNN-based deep learning models.

3

Model Inference :

Deep learning architectures (MobileNet, EfficientNet, ResNet, DenseNet) perform automated cancer detection and subtype classification. Results are generated as probability scores, not definitive diagnoses.

4

Explainability Analysis :

Grad-CAM heatmaps are generated to highlight image regions that influenced the AI predictions.

5

Report Generation :

A secure, downloadable PDF report is generated containing predictions, confidence scores, visualizations, and AI-driven recommendations.

Processing & Security : All analysis occurs in a secure, isolated, and encrypted environment with access controls. Processing follows medical imaging best practices and is typically completed within minutes.

Data Security & Storage

1 Storage Infrastructure :

Medical images and AI analysis results are stored on secure, encrypted cloud servers aligned with healthcare data protection standards. Data centers use redundancy and controlled access mechanisms to ensure availability and integrity.

2 Encryption Standards :

  • In Transit: All data transmissions are protected using HTTPS/TLS (TLS 1.3).
  • At Rest: Stored images, reports, and analysis data are encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Database & Backups: Encrypted databases and secured backups with controlled key management are maintained for additional protection.

3 Access Control & Security :

Access is restricted to authorized systems and authenticated users. Audit logs may record access, views, and downloads for security monitoring.

4 Retention & Deletion :

Data is retained for a defined retention period in accordance with medical imaging standards or account requirements, after which it is securely deleted. Users may request earlier deletion where applicable.

5 Data Sharing :

Identifiable data is not shared with third parties except when strictly necessary for infrastructure or legal compliance. Anonymized data, if used for research, will not contain personal identifiers.

AI Limitations & Medical Disclaimer

⚠️

Important Limitations :

While CancerVision provides valuable diagnostic support, users must understand its limitations.

  • Assistive, Not Diagnostic : CancerVision provides AI-assisted analysis and does not offer standalone medical diagnoses. All findings must be reviewed by a licensed medical professional.
  • Model Accuracy & Variability : Model performance may vary depending on data quality and unseen clinical scenarios.
  • False Positives & False Negatives : The system may produce incorrect predictions, including false positives or false negatives.
  • Image Quality Dependency : Results depend on proper image acquisition and scan quality.
  • Limited Scope : The system is trained on specific cancer types and may not detect rare or untrained conditions.
  • No Emergency Use : CancerVision is not intended for emergency or acute clinical decision-making.
  • Continuous Model Updates : Model improvements may lead to variations in outputs over time.
  • Research Use (Optional) : Users may opt in to allow the use of fully anonymized images and model outputs for research and system improvement. Participation is voluntary and may be withdrawn at any time.
  • Children & Guardian Consent : For patients below the legal age of majority, consent must be provided by a parent or legal guardian.
  • Data Breach Notification : In the event of a data breach, affected users and relevant authorities will be notified in accordance with applicable laws.
Clinical Responsibility : Final responsibility for diagnosis, interpretation, and treatment decisions remains solely with the treating physician. CancerVision is intended to support clinical judgment, not replace it.
