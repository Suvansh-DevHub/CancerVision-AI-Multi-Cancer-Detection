Understanding Grad-CAM

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) is a technique that highlights the regions of the image that were most influential in the model's decision-making process.

What this shows:
• Red/warm colors = High model focus (strong indicators)
• Blue/cool colors = Low model focus (minimal influence)
• This heatmap helps validate that the model is looking at the right regions of the scan
