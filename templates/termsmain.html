What CancerVision Does

CancerVision is an AI-powered tool designed to assist in analyzing medical images for potential signs of cancer. Think of us as a supportive second opinion – we use advanced machine learning to examine images and highlight areas that may warrant closer attention from medical professionals.

Our service provides visual analysis including confidence scores and heatmaps to help healthcare providers make more informed decisions. We're here to support the diagnostic process, not replace it.

In addition, CancerVision offers advanced clinician-focused features:

  • Subtype Classification: Categorizes detected abnormalities into clinically meaningful subtypes to support deeper diagnostic evaluation.
  • Downloadable PDF Reports: Generates exportable, clinician-ready reports summarizing analysis, confidence scores, and heatmaps for medical review or documentation.
ℹ️

CancerVision is intended as a clinical decision-support tool for use by qualified healthcare professionals (radiologists, oncologists and treating clinicians) to assist in the interpretation of medical images (CT, X-ray, MRI, mammography, dermoscopy). Understanding Your Results

Our analysis comes with two key visual components to help you understand what we've found:

Confidence Scores
These are percentage values (0–100%) indicating how certain our AI is about its analysis. A higher score means our model detected patterns more strongly associated with the condition we’re analyzing for. Important: Even high confidence scores require professional medical interpretation and should never be used in isolation.
Heatmaps
Visual overlays showing which areas of the image our AI focused on during analysis. Warmer colors (red, orange) indicate regions that contributed more to the prediction. These help medical professionals understand AI’s “reasoning” but are interpretive tools, not definitive markers of disease location.

Remember: Our AI can make mistakes. False positives and false negatives are possible. Results must always be validated by qualified medical professionals using comprehensive diagnostic methods.
