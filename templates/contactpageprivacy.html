Privacy & Data Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy & Data Policy applies only to the Contact / Support form of Cancer Vision. It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it's protected.

Introduction :

Cancer Vision is a research-based AI tool that assists with image-based cancer detection for educational use. It is not a medical device. Always consult a professional for medical advice.

Information We Collect :

Through our Contact / Support form, we collect:

  • Form details: name, email, phone number, preferred contact method, and message content.
  • Optional details: organization, role, address, website/LinkedIn, uploaded attachments (images or PDFs for context).

We do not collect any medical data, health records, or biometric information through this form.

Purpose of Data Collection :

  • Respond to your inquiry or support request.
  • Provide follow-up communication.
  • Handle partnership or collaboration requests.
  • Maintain internal records to improve service quality.

How We Store and Protect Your Data :

  • All submissions sent securely over HTTPS.
  • Data stored on secure cloud servers (e.g., Google Cloud / Firebase).
  • Access limited to authorized support members only.
  • Uploaded files are deleted automatically within 30 days unless needed for support follow-up.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information publicly.

Data Sharing :

  • Shared only with internal team members handling your query.
  • Shared with service providers (hosting/email) under confidentiality agreements.
  • Shared only if legally required by authorities.

Your Rights :

  • Request a copy or deletion of your submitted data anytime.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Contact us for privacy concerns at: cancervision.helpdesk@gmail.com

Cookies & Tracking :

The Contact page does not use cookies or tracking. General website analytics (if active) collect anonymous visit data only and are not linked to contact form submissions.

Policy Updates :

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version is always available at /docs/privacy.html.
